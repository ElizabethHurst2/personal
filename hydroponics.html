WATER SYSTEMS

Hydroponics and rain harvesting systems built at various New York City public schools.

WBCHS MOBILE SCIENCE GARDEN

This mobile hydroponics garden was designed and built with West Brooklyn Community High School Science Interns. We developed the design through a collaborative design process. The structure was conceptualized as a life sciences teaching tool, space to hang out, and semi-private sanctuary. The garden lives in the school cafeteria. Beam Center, 2017.

LONG WALK TO WATER GARDEN

Rain water harvesting shed and garden built with middle school students from Queens, NY. The shed was inspired by the book Long Walk to Water, by Linda Sue Park. It incorporated a series of hand painted images on wheels that were activated by rainfall. Beam Center, 2016.
