COMPOST FOR PRESIDENT

100% of proceeds from Compost for President projects go towards fighting the construction of pipelines on stolen land.

RealCompost, 2024

T-shirt design made from real compost and screenprinted onto cotton. Naturally dyed using turmeric, onion skin, indigo, madder, coffee, chestnut, chamomile, hibiscus and more. Cyanotypes made using roots and dead bugs. $50-80 sliding scale.


I PLEDGE ALLEGIENCE TO THE FLOWERS, 2022

Cyanotype print using pressed wildflowers and homemade negatives. Cyanotype photography is a chemical process that uses sunlight and water to develop images. Each print is unique and imperfect! Approximate size is 11" x 25". This piece was auctioned as part of Catalyst at Compere Collective and Let's Get Free's Picturing a Free World, both in 2023. Pay what you wish.


TRUST TOKENS, 2021

Ceramic tokens made using homemade stamp, colored pigment and various stoneware clays. Each token is unique and imperfect!
Some ideas for sharing tokens: Hide them for children to find. Give them to friends, lovers, neighbors and family. Slip them into the tip jar of a cute barista (plus a tip of course). Bury them.

Tokens are approximately 1.5" in diameter. $10 for a bag of ten. Tokens were auctioned as part of Catalyst at Compere Collective and Let's Get Free's Picturing a Free World, both in 2023.

COMPOST FOR PRESIDENT, 2020 and 2023
