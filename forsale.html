I PLEDGE ALLEGIENCE TO THE FLOWERS, 2023

Cyanotype print using pressed wildflowers and homemade negatives. Cyanotype photography is a chemical process that uses sunlight and water to develop images. Each print is unique and imperfect! Approximate size is 11" x 25". This piece was auctioned as part of Catalyst at Compere Collective and Let's Get Free's Picturing a Free World, both in 2023.


TRUST TOKENS, 2021

Ceramic tokens made using homemade stamp, colored pigment and various stoneware clays. Each token is unique and imperfect!
Some ideas for sharing tokens: Hide them for children to find. Give them to friends, lovers, neighbors and family. Slip them into the tip jar of a cute barista (plus a tip of course). Bury them.

Tokens are approximately 1.5" in diameter. $10 for a bag of ten.

RECYCLED PLASTIC KEYCHAINS, 2018

Keychains made from melted laundry detergent bottles pressed together and cut into fragments. Each keychain is different colors and shapes. $5 each.

COMPOST FOR PRESIDENT, 2020

Compost for President campaign invented during the 2020 election season (co-created with Eli Nixon).
T-shirts and patches made using cyanotype chemicals and homemade negatives on cotton fabric. Patches are backed with felt and are approximately 3.5" in diameter. Patches are $5.
