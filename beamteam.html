BEAM TEAM

Beam Team is an after school format for middle schoolers that was designed and first created in 2016 in collaboration Allen Riley, Jeff Wood, and many other minds. The concept for Beam Team is that we offer a theme, technical guidance, a bunch of odds and ends to play with, and a series of connected prompts and students work in pairs to brainstorm, design and build out ideas. At the end of the program, we invite family and friends to come experience and celebrate what's been made.

TELECOMMUNICATION

The 2017 Beam Team theme was telecommunication. We made slime pumps, analog copy machines, PVC airhorns, pneumatic pianos, pom pom whisperers, and much more.
