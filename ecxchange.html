ECXCHANGE

The ECXchange Container was a free shop for art supplies, recyclable materials and unwanted artwork at Edinburgh College of Art. With the help of the Student Representative Council, a Waste Action grant secured the rental of a shipping container in the ECA courtyard for one full year, which I built into a supplies shop.

In addition, the ECX was a web-based local exchange system I created within the Edinburgh College of Art to help students share tools and materials, look for help, make new connections and find ways of learning. Edinburgh, 2009-2010.
