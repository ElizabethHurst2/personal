COSMOS

As part of Cosmos themed daycamp, we made slimey specimens and wrote up scientific reports on their behaviors. Then we made our own microscope cameras by taking apart and inverting the lenses of salvaged webcams and housing them in recycled plastic bottles and duct tape. We finished the project by documenting our slime specimens with our microscope cameras and presenting our research to the group. Beam Center, 2016.
